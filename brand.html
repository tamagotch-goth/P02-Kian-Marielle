About Space Futura

Space Futura is about all things retro-futuristic. We sell antiques and memorabilia that have a blend of retro-styles and futuristic technology – think the Jetsons or even Katsuhiro Otomo’s Akira. We showcase how those of the past saw our future and revel in the inventive and zany technologies they imagined for us.

What We Sell

Antique retro-futuristic items, recreated retro toys, retro t-shirts and clothes, books, and posters (everything retro themed).

Selling Point

There are no shops like this on the internet. It’s a one of a kind shop, only rivaled by Esty shops. It consolidates all retro-futuristic items in one place.
