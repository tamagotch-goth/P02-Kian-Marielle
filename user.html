Space Futura Customers

At Space Futura we are selling items based off how the past imagined the future. We love the past, but we really love how the past imagine our future (and the graphic design they used ). Our customers are often 30+ history or sci-fi buffs - selfproclaimed "nerds", if you will. We want to meet peoples needs of cool retro space themed items, all while packaging everything in really cool retro designs.

User Goals
